About Us

Know about our story

My name is Abhishek Pandey, and I'm a full-time practicing trader in the financial market with over two years of experience. While my professional journey in trading has been rewarding, my passion for fashion has always been a part of me. I've known Shubham Phaphale since 2019, when we both started our first jobs. Shubham, who hails from a small village and completed his ITI course as a machinist, was a new face in the company. We quickly became friends through a mutual connection, and over time, our bond has grown stronger. Shubham's dedication and hardworking nature have inspired me in many ways, and together, we decided to bring my long-time vision of a clothing brand to life.

Our Vision

The vision behind our clothing brand is simple: "To create a name that represents high-quality clothing at affordable prices, with fresh, trendy, and fashionable designs that stay ahead of the curve". This idea has been brewing in my mind since my college days, but it’s only now that I have the courage and the drive to turn it into reality. Through our brand, we want to offer clothing that not only looks good but also feels good, combining style with comfort. With Shubham’s support and our shared determination, we are committed to building a brand that stands for quality, affordability, and continuous innovation in fashion.
